Access and public transport in the Confluence district

Perrache, 7 trees planted in November

A few months after the inauguration of the France Pejot passage, we are planting seven trees at the foot of Perrache station on the south side. This is the final stage of the first phase of the Perrache metamorphosis begun in 2018.

T2 is coming to La Confluence!

From 24 March 2021, after the final tests, the T2 tramway line will be extended to La Confluence. The new terminus will now be at the Hôtel de Région - Montrochet station, a joint stop with the T1 line.

Pedestrianisation of the Cours Charlemagne.

Since spring 2021, the Metropolis and the City of Lyon have been experimenting with pedestrianisation on Cours Charlemagne, between Quai Riboud and Rue Montrochet. See you on 18 October at 6.30pm to find out the results.

Towards new energy communities!

See you on 2nd July at 11:30am for a live event dedicated to energy at La Confluence in Lyon. A territory of innovations where collective self-consumption is being tested at different scales.
